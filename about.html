About L00King Out

L00King Out is a community driven app that allows businesses to communicate with their local community to help complete tasks for exchange of goods. It elimates the monetary aspect of providing services and the philosophy behind it is to strengthen the bonds. It also gives users an opportunity to grow their business or simply get a task complete as part of their barter.
